Privacy Policy

1. Who we are

MyBadGuy is a free consumer security awareness service operated at mybadguy.com. We are not a security company, a data broker, or an analytics provider. We are an educational tool that shows consumers how threat actors see their devices, and gives them practical steps to reduce their exposure.

Questions about this policy can be directed to support@mybadguy.com.

2. What device detection reads

When you start a detection, your browser automatically provides a small set of technical signals. We read these signals to identify your device type and operating system version. We do not read anything beyond what your browser makes available without permission.

Signal What we use it for Stored?
User-agent string Device type, OS name, OS version Device type, OS, and version only — no raw UA string
Touch point count Distinguish iPad from Mac No
Platform string Distinguish Mac from Windows No
IP address Rate limiting only — max 5 requests per minute No — never written to the database

We do not read your location, microphone, camera, contacts, files, installed apps, browsing history, clipboard, or any other device resource. No permissions are requested.

3. Profile questions

The 10 profile questions ask about your general situation — age range, financial situation, tech comfort, and how you use your device. These questions are intentionally generic. We never ask for your name, address, employer, income, account numbers, or any other personally identifying information.

Your answers are stored locally in your browser's session storage and used immediately to compute your threat scores. If you choose not to save your results, this data is deleted when you close the tab and never reaches our servers.

4. Saved detection results

Saving your results is entirely optional. If you choose to save, we store the following on our servers so you can return and track your progress over time.

Data stored Purpose
Private ID A four-word random phrase that identifies your record. We generate it — you never choose it.
PIN (hashed) A 4-digit PIN of your choosing, stored as a one-way cryptographic hash using PBKDF2-SHA256 with 100,000 iterations. We cannot recover your PIN.
Device type e.g. iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows PC
OS name and version e.g. iOS 26.4.2 — used to track patch status over time
Patch status Current, behind, or outdated — derived from your OS version
Profile answers The 10 numeric scores from your profile questions (e.g. age range: 35–44, financial status: 3/5). No free-text responses are stored.
Detection date When you last ran a detection — used to flag new threats since your last visit
We never store: your name, email, phone number, IP address, location, precise device identifiers (IMEI, serial number, advertising ID), or any information that could identify you as an individual.

Your saved record is protected by your private ID and PIN together. Without both, the record cannot be accessed, modified, or deleted — including by us.

5. Cookies and tracking

MyBadGuy does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use advertising trackers, analytics pixels, social media buttons, or any cross-site tracking technology.

Cloudflare provides our infrastructure and may process your IP address as part of standard network operations (DDoS protection, TLS termination, routing). Cloudflare's data processing is governed by their Privacy Policy. We have configured Cloudflare Analytics in its privacy-first mode, which does not use cookies or fingerprinting and does not share data with third parties.

Google Fonts is loaded from fonts.googleapis.com to render our typefaces. Google may log the request IP address in accordance with their Privacy Policy. No cookies are set by this request.

6. Data retention and deletion

Unsaved sessions leave no data on our servers. Browser session storage is cleared when you close the tab.

Saved records are retained until you delete them. To delete your record permanently, go to the detection page, enter your private ID and PIN, and choose Delete. Deletion is immediate and irreversible — we have no way to recover a deleted record.

We reserve the right to delete inactive saved records after 24 months of no activity. If we do so, we will have no way to notify you as we do not hold your contact details.

7. Your rights

Regardless of where you are in the world, we honour the following rights. Because we do not collect identity information, exercising most of these rights simply requires your private ID and PIN.

  • Right to access. You can retrieve your saved record at any time using your private ID and PIN via the detection page.
  • Right to deletion. You can permanently delete your saved record at any time using your private ID and PIN. No request to us is required.
  • Right to portability. Your saved record is returned in JSON format when you retrieve it. You can copy and store it anywhere.
  • Right to rectification. You can update your record by running a new detection and saving the updated results.
  • Right to object. If you believe we are processing your data inappropriately, contact us at support@mybadguy.com.
  • California residents (CCPA/CPRA). We do not sell or share your personal information. There is nothing to opt out of. You may request confirmation of this in writing by emailing us.
  • EU/UK residents (GDPR/UK GDPR). Our lawful basis for processing saved records is your explicit consent, given when you choose to save your results. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your record.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest. All communication between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.3 with HSTS enforced. PINs are stored using PBKDF2-SHA256 with 100,000 iterations and a per-record salt — they cannot be recovered even by us. Our API enforces rate limiting, strict input validation, and Content Security Policy headers.

No internet-connected system is perfectly secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach we will notify affected users as required by applicable law — though given that we hold no contact information, notification may be limited to a prominent notice on this site.

9. Children

MyBadGuy is available to users aged 13 and older. The minimum age selector in our profile questions is 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created a saved record, contact us at support@mybadguy.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Third-party data sources

Our threat intelligence database is built from publicly available government and research datasets. We do not receive any personal data from these sources. Data is fetched by our servers and stored in our own database — your browser never contacts these third parties directly.

Sources include the NIST National Vulnerability Database, CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog, MITRE ATT&CK, Microsoft Security Response Center, endoflife.date, and PhishTank. See our Data sources page for the full list.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Because we hold no contact information, we cannot notify you directly — we will post changes here and, for material changes, add a notice on the homepage.

Continued use of MyBadGuy after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. If you disagree with a change, you may delete your saved record and discontinue use at any time.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or to report a concern, contact us at support@mybadguy.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

For security vulnerability reports, please follow responsible disclosure and email support@mybadguy.com with details. We do not currently operate a public bug bounty programme.
