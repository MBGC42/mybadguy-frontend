Who threatens you

Multiple actors. Three categories.

Threat actors are profiling people like you right now. Find out which ones are most interested in you — and what to do about it.

Technical threats
Nation-state hackers, insider threats, and data brokers who exploit your device, software vulnerabilities, and online accounts.
Social threats
Scammers and stalkers who exploit trust, isolation, and personal relationships — not technology.
Financial threats
Cybercriminals and opportunistic thieves motivated by direct financial gain through fraud or device theft.

Our principles

How we operate.

Factual
We cite our sources. If we cannot substantiate a claim, we do not make it.
Ethical
We will not shame, manipulate, or alarm users to drive engagement. Scores reflect real risk, not marketing.
Private
We collect only what is necessary, protect it rigorously, and delete it on request. See our Privacy policy.
Transparent
We disclose how our scores are calculated, where our data comes from, and what we do not know.
Accessible
Plain language, mobile-first, and free — always. Security awareness should not require a degree or a subscription.
