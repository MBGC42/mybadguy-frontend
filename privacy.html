Privacy policy

Effective 10 May 2026. We believe privacy policies should be readable by real people, not just lawyers.

Who we are What we read Profile questions Saved results Cookies Deletion Your rights

1. Who we are

MyBadGuy is a free consumer security awareness service operated at mybadguy.com. We are not a security company, a data broker, or an analytics provider. We are an educational tool that shows consumers how threat actors see their devices, and gives them practical steps to reduce their exposure.

Questions about this policy can be directed to support@mybadguy.com.

2. What device detection reads

When you start a detection, your browser automatically provides a small set of technical signals. We read these to identify your device type and OS version. We do not read anything beyond what your browser makes available without permission.

SignalWhat we use it forStored?
User-agent stringDevice type, OS name, OS versionDevice type, OS, and version only — no raw UA string
Touch point countDistinguish iPad from MacNo
Platform stringDistinguish Mac from WindowsNo
IP addressRate limiting onlyNo — never written to the database

We do not read your location, microphone, camera, contacts, files, installed apps, browsing history, or any other device resource. No permissions are requested.

3. Profile questions

The 10 profile questions ask about your general situation — age range, financial situation, tech comfort, and how you use your device. These questions are intentionally generic. We never ask for your name, address, employer, income, account numbers, or any personally identifying information.

Your answers are stored locally in your browser's session storage and used immediately to compute your threat scores. If you choose not to save your results, this data is deleted when you close the tab and never reaches our servers.

4. Saved detection results

Saving your results is entirely optional. If you choose to save, we store the following on our servers so you can return and track your progress over time.

Data storedPurpose
Private IDA four-word random phrase that identifies your record. We generate it — you never choose it.
PIN (hashed)Your 4-digit PIN stored as a one-way cryptographic hash using PBKDF2-SHA256, 100,000 iterations. We cannot recover your PIN.
Device typee.g. iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows PC

We never store: your name, email, phone number, IP address, location, precise device identifiers, or any information that could identify you as an individual.

Your saved record is protected by your private ID and PIN together. Without both, the record cannot be accessed, modified, or deleted — including by us.

5. Cookies and tracking

MyBadGuy does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use advertising trackers, analytics pixels, social media buttons, or any cross-site tracking technology.

Cloudflare provides our infrastructure and may process your IP address as part of standard network operations. We have configured Cloudflare Analytics in privacy-first mode — no cookies, no fingerprinting, no third-party sharing.

Google Fonts is loaded from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log the request IP address per their Privacy Policy. No cookies are set by this request.

6. Data retention and deletion

Unsaved sessions leave no data on our servers. Browser session storage is cleared when you close the tab.

Saved records are retained until you delete them. To delete your record permanently, go to the detection page, enter your private ID and PIN, and choose Delete. Deletion is immediate and irreversible — we have no way to recover a deleted record.

We reserve the right to delete inactive saved records after 24 months of no activity.

7. Your rights

Regardless of where you are in the world, we honour the following rights. Because we do not collect identity information, exercising most of these rights simply requires your private ID and PIN.

  • Right to access. You can retrieve your saved record at any time using your private ID and PIN.
  • Right to deletion. You can permanently delete your saved record at any time. No request to us is required.
  • Right to portability. Your saved record is returned in JSON format when you retrieve it.
  • Right to rectification. You can update your record by running a new detection and saving the updated results.
  • Right to object. If you believe we are processing your data inappropriately, contact us at support@mybadguy.com.
  • California residents (CCPA/CPRA). We do not sell or share your personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • EU/UK residents (GDPR/UK GDPR). Our lawful basis for processing saved records is your explicit consent, given when you choose to save. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your record.

8. Security

All communication uses TLS 1.3 with HSTS enforced. PINs are stored using PBKDF2-SHA256 with 100,000 iterations and a per-record salt — they cannot be recovered even by us. Our API enforces rate limiting, strict input validation, and Content Security Policy headers.

9. Children

MyBadGuy is available to users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created a saved record, contact us at support@mybadguy.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Third-party data sources

Our threat intelligence database is built from publicly available government and research datasets. We do not receive any personal data from these sources. Data is fetched by our servers — your browser never contacts these third parties directly. See our Data sources page for the full list.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. The effective date at the top reflects the most recent revision. For material changes we will add a notice on the homepage.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or to report a concern, contact us at support@mybadguy.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
